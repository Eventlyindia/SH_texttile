Our Craft Philosophy

Every Thread Placed with Intention

We partner directly with weavers and artisans from Gujarat, Rajasthan, and Bengal — ensuring fair wages and authentic craft traditions.

  • All fabrics are naturally dyed using traditional vegetable and mineral pigments
  • Each photo frame is hand-embroidered with silk thread on Khadi base cloth
  • Curtains are woven on handlooms by master craftspeople in Kutch
  • Showpieces are created using centuries-old Dhokra and Zardozi techniques
